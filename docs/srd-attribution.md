## Scope of SRD use in Pendragon

Pendragon uses the SRD 5.1 as the sole legal source for D&D 5e content. We
deliberately avoid any non-SRD material (Tasha's, Xanathar's, Volo's, setting
lore, trademarked names, etc.) to keep redistribution unambiguous under
CC-BY 4.0.

### What we use from the SRD

- **Mechanics:** d20, advantage/disadvantage, ability scores, saving throws,
  attack rolls, proficiency, AC, HP, exhaustion, conditions.
- **Races:** Human, Dwarf (Hill/Mountain), Elf (High/Wood/Dark), Halfling
  (Lightfoot/Stout), Dragonborn, Gnome (Forest/Rock), Half-Elf, Half-Orc,
  Tiefling.
- **Classes:** Barbarian, Bard, Cleric, Druid, Fighter, Monk, Paladin, Ranger,
  Rogue, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard — SRD subclass subset only.
- **Backgrounds:** Acolyte (the only fully-SRD background). Other backgrounds
  shipped with Pendragon are original.
- **Creatures:** the ~300+ monsters listed in the SRD 5.1.
- **Spells:** the spell list included in the SRD 5.1.

### What we do NOT use

- Trademarked names: "Dungeons & Dragons", "D&D", "Forgotten Realms",
  "Wizards of the Coast" outside attribution lines.
- Settings: Faerûn, Eberron, Ravnica, etc.
- Post-2014 mechanics absent from the SRD 5.1.
- Any proprietary lore.
